Welcome to Keys!

Have you ever wondered how to play a tune like “Happy Birthday” on the piano? This is the site for you!
Keys' purpose is to teach simple tunes by guiding you, note by note, through a tune.
As an added feature, you can record your own tune to show off or even teach to others!

Instructions

Rotate your screen if your are using a mobile device. After clicking on on this page you will be taken to the simple piano. There you can:

Select a Tune

Choose your tune, or the "Record your own!" option.

Play the Tune

Click to play the tune. Clicking on the button again will restart the selected tune.

Teach the Selected Tune

Follow the displayed notes on the keyboard. If the last note you played was wrong, try the displayed note again.

Record

When “Record your own!” is selected this button will appear. When clicked all keys you click will be recorded. To Stop recording press Stop Recording. Your tune is now saved and can be played back or taught.

Stop

The stop button will stop any tunes being taught or being played.

Settings

By clicking on the settings button, you can change the sounds the piano makes as well as display the names of the notes of the keys.

Please Note

The Keys piano works on all modern browsers such as Firefox, Chrome, Opera, Safari. If you are unable to view the piano, consider upgrading your browser.
